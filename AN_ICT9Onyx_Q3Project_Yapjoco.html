Ways to prevent fires occuring

As part of the Fire Prevention Month in March, we have given ways and guides on how to prevent the possibilities and chances of there being a fire accident. Fires can spread easily when left neglected, examples can be wildfires, large amounts of smoke and toxic elements, as well as it being very detrimental to the environmen. In this webpage, I'll tell you the ways on how to prevent fire.

 Check the electrical cords Check your electrical cords in your house for signs of fraying and faultiness, if there are signs of these then replace the ones which are faulty
 Carelessness in the Kitchen Carelessness in the Kitchen may lead to house fire. Leaving your pots or pans unattended on the stove may lead to a fire, not keeping your stove and oven clean may lead to a fire, since the built-up food splatter and grease might ignite when the stove is turned on.
 Clothes Dryers Cleaning out your dryer vent will help with the less chances of a fire, cleaning out the lint filter will also help since the lint can catch on fire.
 Flammable Products Keep and gasoline, paint thinners, and other highly flammable liquids or materials safely, you can put these in UL apporved containers and you can keep them out of the house for less chances of fire
 Extension Cords Using and/or overloading an extension cord for extend periods of time can cause fires, you have to be careful on the usage of extension cords.
